Freshen Up

Freshen Up is a company that offers eco-friendly shower products. Freshen up offers Shampoos, soaps, conditioners, and lotion all of which are zero waste, and made with natural ingredients. The company also offers refills to reduce the plastic waste. There are also a variety of scents that customers can choose to their liking such as peaches, and eucalyptes for example.
